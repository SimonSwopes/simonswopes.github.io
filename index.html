Texas Tech University: Computer Science

I am an Undergraduate in Computer Science who is interested in making impactful software. I learned my first programming language at an engineering camp when I was 13. As I approach my comment day on August 3rd I am looking forward to not only applying these skills on my personal projects, but using them to make a meaningful impact on the world around me.
